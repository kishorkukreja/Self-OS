# Rowboat

**Type:** tool

**Description:** Rowboat is an open-source local AI second brain that stores a knowledge graph in Markdown/Obsidian-compatible form.

**Key facts:**
- Ingests sources such as Gmail, Calendar, and meeting transcripts, then extracts typed decisions and commitments into local Markdown.
