chiphuyen/aie book is Chip Huyen’s public repository of resources for AI engineers and supporting materials for her 2025 book AI Engineering . It is a high signal reference for building real world applications with foundation models, covering the full lifecycle from deciding whether to build an AI application through evaluation, prompting, RAG, agents, finetuning, dataset engineering, inference optimization, architecture, and user feedback loops. Book positioning AI Engineering focuses on the end to end process of adapting foundation models to solve real world problems. Foundation models include: Large language models ( LLMs ) Large multimodal models ( LMMs ) The book combines: established engineering practices, emerging techniques specific to foundation models, practical production lessons, case studies and real world examples, evaluation, deployment, optimization, and feedback loop concerns. Chip explicitly positions the book around fundamentals: Tools become outdated quickly, but fundamentals should last longer. It is not primarily a tutorial/code snippet book.
