**Summary:** WUPHF ("woof") — Slack for AI Employees captures a repo relevant to AI engineering workflows, agent infrastructure, and knowledge-management practices. The source is useful because it records concrete capabilities, setup details, and design trade-offs that can be compared against the rest of the AI Research OS corpus. WUPHF ("woof") — Slack for AI Employees "Slack for AI employees with a shared brain." A collaborative office that runs 24/7 where AI agents (CEO, PM, engineers, designer, CMO, CRO) are visible, claim tasks, argue, and ship work—instead of disappearing behind an API. Unlike accumulated-session orchestrators, WUPHF uses fresh sessions per turn, per-agent scoped tools, and push-driven agent wakes for zero idle burn.
