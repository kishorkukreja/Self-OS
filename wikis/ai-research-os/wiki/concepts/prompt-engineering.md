# Prompt Engineering

**Definition:** The discipline of crafting and optimising text inputs to large language models so they produce accurate, useful, and well-formatted outputs.

**Why it matters:** Prompt engineering is the primary interface between humans and modern LLMs. Small changes in wording, structure, or context can dramatically shift model performance. It encompasses techniques such as chain-of-thought, few-shot examples, persona assignment, and output-schema enforcement.
