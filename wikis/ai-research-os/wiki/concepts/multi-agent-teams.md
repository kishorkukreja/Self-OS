# Multi-Agent Teams

**Definition:** Multi-agent teams let agents communicate with each other directly under roles established by a main coordinator.

**Why it matters:** The pattern can keep the main agent context clean, but creates infrastructure needs around deadlock, edit conflicts, and completion reporting.
