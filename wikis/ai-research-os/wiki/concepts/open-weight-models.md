# Open-weight Models

**Definition:** Open-weight models expose model weights for external use, inspection, or deployment, though licenses and training data transparency vary.

**Why it matters:** ZAYA1-8B and the broader 2026 open-model discussion show open-weight models competing not only on quality but on hardware independence, licensing, and inference economics.
