# Self-Hosted Deep Research

**Definition:** Self-hosted deep research combines retrieval, orchestration, and report writing on infrastructure controlled by the user or organization.

**Why it matters:** The digest positions Onyx and related stacks as credible options when IP, data residency, or supplier intelligence cannot be sent to closed-cloud systems.
