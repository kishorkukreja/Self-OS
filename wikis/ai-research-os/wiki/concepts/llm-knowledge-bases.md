# LLM Knowledge Bases

**Definition:** LLM knowledge bases are AI-assisted personal or organizational knowledge stores assembled and queried with language models.

**Why it matters:** Karpathy’s X-native signal fits Self-OS directly: LLMs can help build topic-specific research maps when raw evidence and compiled pages stay separated.
