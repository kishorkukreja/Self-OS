# World Models

**Definition:** World models learn representations that predict how environments change in response to actions.

**Why it matters:** The Code issue frames world models as a major alternative or complement to language-only prediction, especially for physical-world AI and multimodal agents. The practical bottleneck is action-labelled data: systems need examples that connect perception, intervention, and consequence.
