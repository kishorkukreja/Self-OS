# Embodied AI

**Embodied AI** extends LLM and VLM capabilities into physical environments where agents must perceive, predict, interact, and plan. Unlike general VLMs trained on static web data, embodied models require fine-grained spatial perception and action-oriented reasoning. [[HY-Embodied-0.5]] exemplifies this direction by training vision-language-action models with dedicated spatial and temporal curricula.

Key capabilities include:
- **Spatial and temporal visual perception:** Understanding 3D layouts, object relationships, and dynamics.
- **Embodied reasoning:** Prediction, interaction planning, and task decomposition in physical space.
- **Edge deployment:** Efficient variants (e.g., MoT-2B) enable real-time robot control on device.
