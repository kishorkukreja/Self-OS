# Small Language Model Agents

**Definition:** Small language model agents use specialized smaller models for narrow, repetitive subtasks inside a broader agentic system.

**Why it matters:** NVIDIA’s argument in the digest is that heterogeneous SLM/LLM stacks may lower cost and improve reliability for structured worker tasks.
