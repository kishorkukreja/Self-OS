# Model Interpretability

**Definition:** Model interpretability studies how AI systems represent information and produce behavior.

**Why it matters:** Interpretability appears in the X/Twitter digest through Goodfire's parameter-level work. For agents, interpretability is part of reliability engineering: teams need to understand failure modes, hallucination incentives, and internal representations when models are embedded in tools and workflows.
