# Research Request: Unilever Forecast Accuracy & Customer Experience

## Question
How can Unilever improve forecast accuracy for its ecommerce channels on Amazon and Target, and what strategies can enhance customer experience from distribution centers (DC) to both offline and online retail?

## Context
Unilever is facing low forecast accuracy in its ecommerce operations with major retailers Amazon and Target. This impacts inventory positioning, stockouts, and overall customer experience across both digital and physical retail channels. Research should explore root causes of forecast inaccuracy in CPG ecommerce and identify best practices for DC-to-customer fulfillment.

## Output wanted
Deep dive with actionable recommendations on:
- Forecasting methodologies suited for ecommerce vs traditional retail
- DC network design for omnichannel fulfillment
- Customer experience improvements from warehouse to doorstep and store shelf
