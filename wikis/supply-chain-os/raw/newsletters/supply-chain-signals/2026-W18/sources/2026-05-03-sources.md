### US manufacturing sector holds steady in April; input costs hit 4-year high

- **Date:** May 1, 2026
- **Section relevance:** Top Moves; Problem Beneath the Headline; Metrics That Matter; Research Radar

### WTO Global Trade Outlook and Statistics, March 2026

- **URL:** https://www.wto.org/english/res_e/booksp_e/gtos0326_e.pdf
- **Publisher/domain:** WTO
- **Date:** March 2026
- **Section relevance:** Research Radar; Big Signal; Metrics That Matter
- **Extracted summary:** WTO forecasts merchandise trade volume growth slowing from 4.6% in 2025 to 1.9% in 2026 before rising to 2.6% in 2027. Services trade growth is projected at 4.8% in 2026. WTO estimates high oil prices linked to the Middle East conflict could shave 0.5 percentage points off 2026 merchandise trade growth and 0.7 percentage points off services growth. AI-related investment contributed almost half of 2025 merchandise trade growth.
- **Extraction notes:** PDF extracted successfully. Authoritative research source.
