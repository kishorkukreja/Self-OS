### UP, NS refile merger applications with STB

- **URL:** https://www.supplychaindive.com/news/up-ns-refile-merger-applications-with-stb/818977/
- **Publisher/domain:** Supply Chain Dive
- **Date:** April 30, 2026
- **Section relevance:** Top Moves; What to Watch Next; possible rail chain deep dive
- **Extracted summary:** Union Pacific and Norfolk Southern refiled an amended merger application with the Surface Transportation Board. The application adds traffic data from six North American Class I railroads after the original filing was rejected as incomplete. The proposed combined network would exceed 50,000 route miles across 43 states. UP/NS claim shipper savings of $3.5 billion and removal of 2.1 million trucks from roads; opponents warn of higher rates, reduced competition, labor risk, and systemic supply-chain harm. Completeness comments are due May 8.
