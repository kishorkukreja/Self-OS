Weekly research folder for Supply Chain Signals.

- `sources/` — source map, extraction notes, chart-use notes.
- `brief/` — Sunday synthesis drafts and social derivatives when generated.

## Editorial working thesis

Start the week with **Wafer to Rack** as the preferred Chain of the Week unless the week's news points more strongly to another chain. Early signals are pointing to a cost/lead-time squeeze: U.S. manufacturing is expanding, but prices and supplier deliveries are worsening; tariff policy is still moving; ocean demand looks uneven; and logistics operators are turning AI/robotics into operating infrastructure rather than pilots.
