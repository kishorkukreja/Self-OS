- Date: 2026-05-07
- Section relevance: Research Radar; Problem Beneath the Headline; From Cost Center to Advantage
- Extracted summary: UNCTAD argues that non-tariff measures such as mandatory standards, regulations, product requirements, and administrative procedures now drive most trade costs. Caveat: report-level estimates.
