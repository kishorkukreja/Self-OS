## S4 — FAO Food Price Index, April 2026 release

- **URL:** https://www.fao.org/worldfoodsituation/foodpricesindex/en/
- **Publisher/domain:** FAO / fao.org
- **Date:** 2026-05-08
- **Section relevance:** Top Moves; Research Radar; Metrics That Matter; Chain of the Week backup: Farm to Fork.

- **Publisher/domain:** WTO / wto.org
- **Date:** March 2026; data cutoff 2026-03-10
- **Section relevance:** Research Radar; Big Signal; Metrics That Matter; Wafer-to-Rack macro context.
- **Extracted summary:** WTO forecast world merchandise trade volume growth to slow from 4.6% in 2025 to 1.9% in 2026, then rise to 2.6% in 2027. - **Extraction notes:** PDF extracted successfully but summarized/truncated. Primary research source.
- **Usable quantitative data for chart/graph:** Yes. Trade growth forecast table and downside/upside scenario deltas.
