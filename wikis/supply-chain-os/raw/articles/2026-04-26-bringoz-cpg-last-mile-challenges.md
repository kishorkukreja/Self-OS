    10|# How CPG Brands are Overcoming Last-Mile Delivery Challenges
    11|
    12|**Source:** [Bringoz](https://www.bringoz.com/how-cpg-brands-are-overcoming-last-mile-delivery-challenges/)  
    13|**Published:** September 23, 2025  
    14|**Reading Time:** ~3 min
    15|
    16|---
    17|
    18|## Overview
    19|
    20|For consumer packaged goods (CPG) companies, last-mile delivery is typically the **most complex and expensive leg of the supply chain**. Whether stocking brick-and-mortar shelves, fulfilling direct-to-consumer subscriptions, or meeting eCommerce delivery windows, the final leg significantly impacts both **cost and customer experience**. With rising consumer expectations for speed and convenience, CPG brands must rethink their approach to this critical stage.
    21|
    22|---
    23|
    24|## 1. Build Flexible Delivery Networks
    25|
    26|CPG companies are constructing agile delivery ecosystems using a mix of:
    27|- **In-house drivers**
    28|- **Independent contractors**
    29|- **Third-party logistics (3PL) providers**
    30|
    31|### Benefits of Flexible Fleets
    32|- **Agility:** Easily adapt to delivery delays and demand fluctuations
    33|- **Customer Experience Control:** Managing deliveries in-house allows brands to own service quality and optimize resource allocation
    34|- **Scalability:** Partnering with 3PLs or specialized carriers provides access to established networks, technology, and infrastructure
    35|
    36|### Market Expansion
    37|Local carriers bring the experience and resources needed to help CPG companies **expand faster into new markets** while minimizing costs and maintaining last-mile flexibility.
    38|
    39|---
    40|
    41|## 2. Invest in Warehouse Automation
    42|
    43|To accelerate order fulfillment, brands are investing heavily in **urban-centric warehouse automation**.
    44|
    45|### Key Advantages
    46|- Process orders more quickly
    47|- Increase operational efficiency
    48|- Overcome **labor shortages**
    49|
    50|### Case Study: Unilever
    51|> **Unilever** is prioritizing increasing warehouse automation within its ice cream business.
    52|>
    53|> Highly automated **"dark" warehouses** using technology like **drones for inventory management** will become increasingly important in cold chain logistics, particularly to offset recruiting challenges, according to **Sandeep Desai**, EVP and Chief Product Supply Chain Officer for the company’s ice cream business.
    54|
    55|**Key Statistic:** Within Unilever’s ice cream business, about **10% of its warehouses are currently dark**, and the company aims to grow that percentage in the next several years.
    56|
    57|---
    58|
    59|## 3. Route Optimization and Real-Time Visibility
    60|
    61|### The Problem
    62|Many CPG companies suffer from:
    63|- Lack of **real-time visibility**
    64|- Weak route optimization
    65|- No data insights to evaluate performance
    66|- **Static routing and manual route planning**, leading to time-consuming tasks and operational inefficiencies
    67|
    68|### The Solution
    69|**Delivery management technologies** automate and streamline operations while improving productivity and transparency.
    70|
    71|#### Advanced Route Optimization
    72|Algorithms consider multiple factors simultaneously:
    73|- Traffic conditions
    74|- Delivery time-windows
    75|- Driver schedules
    76|- Vehicle capacity
    77|
    78|> *"Advanced route optimization algorithms can consider various factors including traffic, delivery time-windows, driver schedules, and vehicle capacity to create the most efficient routes."*
    79|
    80|#### Real-Time Adjustments
    81|- Route optimization enables managers to **adjust routes on the fly** in response to traffic, cancellations, or last-minute orders
    82|- This minimizes delivery delays while **maximizing resource utilization**
    83|
    84|#### Visibility & Customer Experience
    85|- **Delivery management platforms** provide a real-time view of the entire last-mile operation
    86|- **Real-time tracking tools** give customers visibility into order location and ETA, helping to reduce failed deliveries and improve satisfaction
    87|- **Data analytics and reporting** provide actionable insights to improve delivery performance, optimize resources, and enhance customer experiences
    88|
    89|---
    90|
    91|## Strategic Outlook
    92|
    93|As sales channels fragment across **retail stores, eCommerce websites, and marketplaces**, the last mile has become an increasingly crowded space.
    94|
    95|**Winning Formula:** Brands that combine a **smarter delivery network** with **flexible capacity** and **real-time delivery orchestration** will be best positioned to meet last-mile expectations and growing demand in the years ahead.
