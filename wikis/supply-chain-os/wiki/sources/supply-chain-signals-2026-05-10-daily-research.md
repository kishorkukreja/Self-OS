**Summary:** The daily research note synthesizes the week’s candidate signals into an operator-focused view. It argues that stable headline manufacturing demand is masking deteriorating internals: input prices, supplier deliveries, tariff deadlines, food-price pressure, and mixed freight conditions are all changing planning assumptions. The note chooses Farm to Fork as the chain of the week because energy pressure is traveling through food inputs, processing, packaging, cold chain, freight, retail, and consumer substitution. It also keeps Wafer to Rack on the watchlist as an AI infrastructure chain constrained by memory, packaging, power, cooling, and construction. The operational recommendation is a live constraint map with owners and decision windows.
