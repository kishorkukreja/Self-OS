# Unilever Strengthens Supply Chain with Amazon via Kaizen Collaboration

**Type:** article  
**Date:** 2026-04-26  
**Client:** internal  
**Confidential:** false  
**Raw file:** [[../raw/articles/2026-04-26-unilever-kaizen-amazon-supply-chain.md]]

**Summary:** Since July 2022, [[entities/unilever.md|Unilever]] and [[entities/amazon.md|Amazon]] have applied [[concepts/kaizen.md|Kaizen]] continuous-improvement workshops across Australia, Brazil, Europe, Egypt, India, and the US. Cross-functional teams map end-to-end pain points, perform data-driven waste reduction, and align on actions. The collaboration has improved 15 KPIs and solved over 100 pain points, including a 40% out-of-stock reduction in Australia and a 55% defect reduction in India.

**Key takeaways:**
- Biannual, in-person Kaizen workshops build trust and surface operational friction.
- A new internal dashboard shares global supply-chain performance data across teams.
- Packaging changes eliminated re-packing steps at Amazon fulfilment centres.
- Both companies are recognised as Gartner Supply Chain Masters.
