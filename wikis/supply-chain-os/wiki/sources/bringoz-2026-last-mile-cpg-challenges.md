# Bringoz: How CPG Brands are Overcoming Last-Mile Delivery Challenges

**Type:** article  
**Date:** 2026-04-26  
**Client:** internal  
**Confidential:** false  
**Raw file:** [[../raw/articles/2026-04-26-bringoz-cpg-last-mile-challenges.md]]

**Summary:** The article argues that [[concepts/last-mile-delivery.md|last-mile delivery]] is the most complex and expensive leg of the CPG supply chain. It recommends building flexible fleets (in-house drivers, contractors, and [[concepts/3pl.md|3PL]] providers)), investing in urban-centric [[concepts/warehouse-automation.md|warehouse automation]] such as dark warehouses, and deploying delivery-management platforms for advanced [[concepts/route-optimization.md|route optimisation]] and real-time visibility.

**Key takeaways:**
- Flexible networks let brands scale capacity up or down while controlling customer experience.
- Unilever’s ice-cream business operates ~10% dark warehouses and plans to grow that share.
- Advanced route algorithms factor traffic, time windows, driver schedules, and vehicle capacity.
- Real-time tracking and data analytics reduce failed deliveries and improve satisfaction.
