## Summary
This source file collects the evidence base for the 2026-05-04 Supply Chain Signals daily research. The main pattern is that supply-chain pressure is rising from a low base while input-cost and delivery indicators are already flashing. Reuters/ISM manufacturing data show expansion in headline PMI, but the more operationally relevant details are high prices paid, slower supplier deliveries, and orders that may include preemptive buying rather than clean demand. New York Fed pressure-index movement supports the same directional story.
