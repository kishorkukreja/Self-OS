# Prologis: Top 8 Omnichannel Logistics Challenges

**Type:** industry-report  
**Date:** 2026-04-26  
**Client:** internal  
**Confidential:** false  
**Raw file:** [[../raw/industry-reports/2026-04-26-prologis-omnichannel-logistics-challenges.md]]

**Summary:** [[entities/prologis.md|Prologis]] identifies eight friction points in omnichannel logistics: lack of inventory and transit visibility, disconnected processes, inefficient order processing, wrong shipping solutions, inefficient return logistics, manual inventory management, and poor 3PL fit. It stresses that omnichannel logistics synchronises inventory, logistics, and distribution across every sales channel and stakeholder.

**Key takeaways:**
- Real-time inventory visibility is essential for next-day delivery promises.
- Machine learning can improve predictive delivery-time accuracy.
- Returns should be accepted in-store for online orders to improve customer experience.
- The right 3PL partner understands the correct metrics and drives continuous improvement.
