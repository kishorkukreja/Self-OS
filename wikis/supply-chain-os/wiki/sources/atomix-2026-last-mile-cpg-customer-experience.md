# Atomix Logistics: How Last-Mile Delivery Impacts CPG Customer Experience

**Type:** article  
**Date:** 2026-04-26  
**Client:** internal  
**Confidential:** false  
**Raw file:** [[../raw/articles/2026-04-26-atomix-last-mile-cpg-customer-experience.md]]

**Summary:** The article positions [[concepts/last-mile-delivery.md|last-mile delivery]] as a strategic determinant of CPG brand reputation and profitability. It recommends partnering with [[concepts/3pl.md|3PL]] providers, deploying order-fulfilment software for real-time tracking, offering same-day and express delivery options, and investing in sustainable solutions such as electric vehicles and [[concepts/micro-fulfillment.md|micro-fulfillment centres]].

**Key takeaways:**
- Last-mile logistics accounts for roughly 53% of total shipping costs.
- AI-powered route optimisation and automated warehousing reduce delays and labour strain.
- Consolidating shipments and using AI-driven demand forecasting curb unnecessary inventory movement.
- Flexible delivery options and real-time tracking increase transparency and reduce missed deliveries.
