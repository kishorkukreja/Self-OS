# Gartner: Future of Supply Chains 2026

**Type:** industry-report  
**Date:** 2026-04-26  
**Client:** internal  
**Confidential:** false  
**Raw file:** [[../raw/industry-reports/2026-04-26-gartner-future-supply-chain-2026.md]]

**Summary:** [[entities/gartner.md|Gartner]] frames the future of supply chain as a shift from operational excellence to commercial innovation. CSCOs must navigate CEO pressure to prove growth partnership, middling GenAI productivity returns, and economic volatility. The five pillars are: supply-chain vision, [[concepts/real-time-visibility.md|real-time execution]], [[concepts/customer-experience.md|customer experience (CX)]], human-centric work and EVP, and sustainable supply chain.

**Key takeaways:**
- 95% of supply chains must react quickly to change, yet only 7% can execute decisions in real time.
- Digital supply-chain investment in real-time decision execution will increase 5x by 2028.
- Enabled customers are 2x as likely to repurchase, but only 23% of supply chains focus on enabling them.
- 63% of organisations expect high competition for talent to continue over the next three to five years.
