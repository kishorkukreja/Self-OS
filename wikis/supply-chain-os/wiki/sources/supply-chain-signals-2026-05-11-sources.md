The May 11 source map collects the evidence base for a week shaped by policy volatility, manufacturing cost pressure, freight divergence, and logistics automation. Reuters items anchor tariff and pharma-network risk; ISM reports provide quantitative manufacturing and services indicators; Supply Chain Dive adds FedEx AI/robotics and warehouse automation signals; FreightWaves contributes truckload, diesel, and trans-Pacific rate context. The source is especially useful because it separates extraction notes, chart-use cautions, and quantitative claims from narrative interpretation. The source is useful primarily as a compiled signal rather than as a final answer: the raw capture remains the canonical place for exact wording, examples, figures, and links.
