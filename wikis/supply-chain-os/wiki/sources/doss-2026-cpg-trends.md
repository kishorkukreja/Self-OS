# DOSS: 5 CPG Industry Trends to Watch in 2026

**Type:** article  
**Date:** 2026-04-26  
**Client:** internal  
**Confidential:** false  
**Raw file:** [[../raw/articles/2026-04-26-doss-cpg-trends-2026.md]]

**Summary:** Based on McKinsey and NVIDIA surveys, the article frames 2026 as a year of operational transformation. Nearly half of CPG executives say their current structure will not survive another decade. The five priority areas are: AI-powered [[concepts/demand-forecasting.md|demand forecasting]], [[concepts/agentic-ai.md|agentic AI]] for autonomous decisions, [[concepts/collaborative-robotics.md|collaborative robotics]] (cobots), [[concepts/sustainable-packaging.md|sustainable packaging]] under EPR regulation, and distributed fulfilment networks.

**Key takeaways:**
- 71% of CPG leaders have integrated AI into at least one function; 51% prioritise supply-chain operational efficiency.
- 47% of retail/CPG firms are using or assessing agentic AI; 20% already have active agents.
- Robotics is seen as the most disruptive technology (63%), with 83% predicting adoption within five years.
- EPR data reporting began in 2025 across several US states, making sustainability an operational and legal imperative.
