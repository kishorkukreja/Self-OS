**Summary:** The weekly brief is the canonical 2026-W19 Supply Chain Signals draft. Its central argument is that the week’s supply-chain story is constraint migration: tariffs, manufacturing cost pressure, supplier-delivery delays, food prices, freight conditions, and AI infrastructure bottlenecks all show the binding constraint moving faster than typical planning cadences. The brief recommends a live constraint map that names the current bottleneck, exposed revenue or service promise, available moves, cost of moves, and decision owner. Farm to Fork becomes the issue’s chain of the week because energy, biofuels, freight, packaging, cold chain, and consumer elasticity are all visible in food-price pressure.
