# Retail Velocity: Retail Intelligence Trends Transforming CPG in 2026

**Type:** article  
**Date:** 2026-04-26  
**Client:** internal  
**Confidential:** false  
**Raw file:** [[../raw/articles/2026-04-26-retail-velocity-retail-intelligence-trends-2026.md]]

**Summary:** CPG companies face a widening gap between those with modernised retail intelligence and those on legacy systems. The report highlights three shifts: (1) [[concepts/agentic-ai.md|agentic AI]] moving from dashboards to autonomous operations; (2) daily SKU/store-level data becoming the minimum viable speed; and (3) true [[concepts/omnichannel-fulfillment.md|omnichannel integration]] requiring a single source of truth across fragmented retailer portals.

**Key takeaways:**
- ~50% of industry professionals are actively using or evaluating agentic AI.
- Real-time sales data is considered crucial by 76% of businesses; data-prepared firms are 52% more likely to grow revenue.
- Retailer data-format incompatibility is the primary technical bottleneck.
- Unified data architectures resolve conflicts and standardise product hierarchies across partners.
