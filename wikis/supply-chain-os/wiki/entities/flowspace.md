# Flowspace

**Type:** tool

**Description:** Technology-driven fulfilment network that unifies inventory, order management, and shipping across sales channels.

**Key facts:**
- Distinguishes true omnichannel (unified, real-time) from multichannel (fragmented, siloed).
- Supports fulfilment from warehouses, retail stores, and micro-fulfillment centres.
- Tracks KPIs such as perfect order rate, order cycle time, inventory turnover, and stockout rate.
