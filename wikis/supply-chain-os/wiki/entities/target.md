# Target

**Type:** retailer

**Description:** Major US retailer serving as both a brick-and-mortar and e-commerce channel for CPG brands.

**Key facts:**
- Operates Partners Online as its vendor data and reporting portal.
- Instituted curbside pickup during the pandemic and allows easy changes to pickup dates/times.
- Frequently used as an example in omnichannel and consumer-experience frameworks.
