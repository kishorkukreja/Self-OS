# Amazon Supply Chain Services

**Type:** service

**Description:** Amazon Supply Chain Services opens Amazon's logistics and supply-chain network to outside businesses.

**Key facts:**
- Reuters coverage cited ocean, road, rail, air, warehousing, fulfillment, inventory forecasting, and two-to-five-day delivery timelines.
- The move is strategically important because it productizes internal logistics scale and forecasting capability for external shippers.
