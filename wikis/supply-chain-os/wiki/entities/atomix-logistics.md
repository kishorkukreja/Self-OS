# Atomix Logistics

**Type:** supplier

**Description:** 3PL and fulfilment partner focused on helping CPG brands optimise last-mile delivery and customer experience.

**Key facts:**
- Positions last-mile logistics as ~53% of total shipping costs.
- Recommends 3PL partnerships, AI route optimisation, real-time tracking, and micro-fulfillment centres.
- Advocates sustainable delivery options such as electric vehicles and bicycle couriers.
