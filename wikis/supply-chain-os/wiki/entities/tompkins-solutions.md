# Tompkins Solutions

**Type:** consultant

**Description:** Consulting firm focused on distribution-centre design, systems integration, and supply-chain strategy for retail and CPG.

**Key facts:**
- Proposes five foundational DC design elements: flexible modular layouts, integrated WMS/OMS/TMS, adaptive robotics, embedded reverse logistics, and real-time data visibility.
- Views the DC as a strategic nerve centre of customer engagement, not just storage.
