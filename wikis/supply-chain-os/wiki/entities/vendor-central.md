# Amazon Vendor Central

**Type:** tool

**Description:** The primary web-based platform through which Amazon manages its first-party (1P) vendor relationships, purchase orders, and demand forecasting.

**Key facts:**
- Generates demand forecasts using 20+ ML and deep-learning models across 50+ geographies.
- Provides ARA Demand Forecast reports with weekly unconstrained demand projections over 26 weeks.
- Uses P-levels (probability levels) to help vendors plan safety stock; higher P-levels mean higher inventory coverage.
- Vendor inputs (promotions, availability, catalog setup, traffic) directly influence ASIN-level forecasts.
