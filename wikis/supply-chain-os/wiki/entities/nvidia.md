# NVIDIA

**Type:** technology-platform

**Description:** Semiconductor and AI computing company that publishes industry survey data on AI adoption in retail and CPG.

**Key facts:**
- 2026 State of AI in Retail and CPG survey found ~50% of professionals actively using or evaluating agentic AI.
- Reported that 64% of respondents faced increased supply-chain challenges year over year.
- 63% of respondents believe robotics has the potential to disrupt or create competitive advantage.
