# MIT Sloan School of Management

**Type:** academic

**Description:** Leading business school with a strong operations-management and supply-chain research programme.

**Key facts:**
- Faculty and students partnered with Unilever to develop a segmented demand-forecasting model using K-medoid clustering and random forests.
- Demonstrated 14–20% accuracy improvement and 60% planning-time reduction.
- Research highlights the value of cluster-while-estimate optimisation for volatile retailer demand patterns.
