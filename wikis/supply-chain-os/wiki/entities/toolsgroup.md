# ToolsGroup

**Type:** tool

**Description:** Supply-chain planning solutions vendor whose analysis of Gartner’s 2026 Magic Quadrant popularised the “decision-centric planning” paradigm.

**Key facts:**
- Treats planning as a continuous “decision repository” rather than a periodic batch exercise.
- Reframed inventory as a network-level decision problem rather than a fixed service/cost trade-off.
- Advocates living segmentation and scenario-driven planning embedded in daily operations.
