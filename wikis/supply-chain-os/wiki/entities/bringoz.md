# Bringoz

**Type:** tool

**Description:** Technology platform specialising in last-mile delivery management, fleet flexibility, and route optimisation for consumer packaged goods.

**Key facts:**
- Recommends mixed fleets (in-house, contractors, 3PLs) to balance agility and cost.
- Supports advanced route optimisation considering traffic, time windows, driver schedules, and vehicle capacity.
- Provides real-time tracking and data analytics to reduce failed deliveries.
