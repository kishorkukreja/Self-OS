# Supplier Diversification

**Definition:** Supplier diversification reduces dependency on any single supplier, region, or tariff exposure, but creates operational control work before resilience is real.

**Why it matters:** The May 6 research note warns that alternate suppliers are not resilient until capacity, quality, documentation, logistics, and commercial terms have been tested under stress.
