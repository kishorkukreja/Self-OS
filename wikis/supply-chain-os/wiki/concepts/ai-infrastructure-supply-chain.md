# AI Infrastructure Supply Chain

**Definition:** The AI infrastructure supply chain spans wafers, memory, CPUs/GPUs, packaging, networking, cooling, power, construction, and data-center operations.

**Why it matters:** The daily research source shows AI demand consuming industrial commons beyond chips, creating allocation and price pressure for non-AI sectors too.
