# Tariff Planning Layer

**Definition:** A tariff planning layer turns tariff exposure into a recurring planning variable alongside demand, capacity, inventory, and freight.

**Why it matters:** Maersk effective-rate estimates and PVH mitigation guidance show tariff exposure becoming a live margin and planning assumption. Ownership may sit across procurement, finance, trade compliance, and S&OP rather than one team.
