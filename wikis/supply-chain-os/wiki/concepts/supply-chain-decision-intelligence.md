# Supply-Chain Decision Intelligence

**Definition:** Supply-chain decision intelligence connects signals, context, policies, thresholds, recommendations, and execution workflows.

**Why it matters:** Logistics Viewpoints and ARC framing emphasize that AI does not improve supply-chain performance merely by adding copilots. Value appears when detection closes the loop to ranked actions, governance, and execution across planning, logistics, sourcing, fulfillment, and risk management.
