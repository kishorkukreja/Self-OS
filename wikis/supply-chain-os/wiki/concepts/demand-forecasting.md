# Demand Forecasting

**Demand forecasting** is the practice of estimating future customer demand using historical data, market signals, and statistical or machine learning models. In CPG and retail, accurate forecasting directly impacts inventory levels, service rates, and working capital. Recent trends integrate AI/ML for segmented forecasting and real-time sensing.
