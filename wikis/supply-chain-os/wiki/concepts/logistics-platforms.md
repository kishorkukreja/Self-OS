# Logistics Platforms

**Definition:** Logistics platforms package physical network density, fulfillment capacity, transportation modes, data, and forecasting into services external customers can buy.

**Why it matters:** Amazon Supply Chain Services is a clear example of a company turning internal logistics scale into an external product. This changes the strategic choice for shippers: what should remain owned or closely controlled, and what can be purchased as infrastructure?
