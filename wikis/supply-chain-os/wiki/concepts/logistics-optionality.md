# Logistics Optionality

**Definition:** Logistics optionality is the ability to switch modes, routes, partners, or fulfillment paths before a disruption becomes an execution failure.

**Why it matters:** Logistics Viewpoints links Ford's flexible manufacturing reset, Hormuz route alternatives, Amazon logistics services, and FourKites execution speed to a broader move away from rigid efficiency. Optionality is increasingly treated as a strategic asset rather than unused redundancy.
